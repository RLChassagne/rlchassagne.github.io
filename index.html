My research

I do computational engineering for Geosciences, working on semi-automated methods to offer a more quantitative qualification and characterisation of the uncertainty in data and models in a merger workflow type of approach. We can call it data integration, data assimilation, or digital twins. My work is real world data-centric driven (deep geothermal, CO2 storage), therefore, aiming to support the decision making process for ressources exploration and exploitation. I develop this approach through different projects I lead or I contribute to, with different research consortia funded by the European council or the French council, which includes industry and academics' partners.
