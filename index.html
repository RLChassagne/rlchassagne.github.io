My research

I do computational engineering for geophysical forecasting  , more specifically, data assimilation, my research interests encompass black-box optimisation problems and related, applied to geophysics in general and more specifically to : 4D Seismic History Matching,  CO2 storage and environmental sciences. I am developing tools and methods to assimilate multi-stream data into the update of models, with regards to the uncertainty. My main concern is how to realize this integration with robustness, accuracy and as fast as possible given the dataset (real dataset driven).
