My research

I do computational engineering for Geosciences, more specifically, data assimilation, my research interests encompass black-box optimisation problems and related predictive tools, applied to field data,   more specifically to geothermal, but not restricted to, I can apply my tools to any subsurface georessource system. I am developing quantitative tools and methods to assimilate multi-stream, multiphysis and multiscale data into model updates, with regards to the uncertainty. My main concern is how to realise this integration with robustness, accuracy and as fast as possible given the datase.

Areas of Interests
  • Quantitative and assisted Geothermal exploration
  • Geothermal monitoring and forecasting
  • Tailored uncertainty characterisation and propagation
  • Generalisation of techniques and methods for georessources (exploration and exploitation)
Problems and methods of Interests
  • Multi-stream data integration (wells, map-based data)
  • Mathematical and computational methods, generalisation and automatisation
  • Uncertainty analysis and propagation
  • Objective function (fitness landscape, metrics, pattern recognition)
