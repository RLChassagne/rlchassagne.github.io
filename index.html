My research

I do computational engineering for geophysics forecasting  , more specifically, my research encompass black-box optimisation problems, applied to geophysics in general and more specifically to : 4D Seismic History Matching,  CO2 storage and environmental sciences. I am developing tools and methods to assimilate multi-sources data into the update of the model, with regards to the uncertainty. My main concern is how to realize this integration with robustness, accuracy and as fast as possible given the dataset (real dataset driven).
