- My research -

I do mathematical-computational engineering for Geosciences, more specifically, my research encompass black-box optimisation problems, applied to 4D Seismic History Matching and CO2 storage. I am developing tools and methods to assimilate multi-sources data into the update of the model, with regards of the uncertainty. My main concern is how to realize this integration with robustness, accuracy and as fast as possible given the dataset (real dataset driven). The main challenge being working with real world dataset. Assistant Professor) at Heriot-Watt University, Edinburgh, UK.

- My areas of interest -

  • Reservoir Characterization and 4D seismic History matching (automation of procedures)

  • Capture Carbon and Storage (monitoring and forecasting)

  • Groundwater contamination (monitoring and forecasting)

- Problems and methods of interest -

  • Multi-sources data integration (production, seismic)

  • Mathematical and computational methods (ML/AI methods to use a buzz word)

  • Uncertainty characterization

  • Objective function (fitness landscape, metrics, pattern recognition)
